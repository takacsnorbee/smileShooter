Smiley Shooter

Let's Start

The main goal is, to earn top scores by shooting down zombies. Do not shoot down the princess pls :) The game is over, if a zombie reach the fence, or the princess is killed. Good luck!
left arrow : hero moves left;

right arrow : hero moves right;

left ctrl : hero shoot;

Press p to pause the game, and press again to continue.
